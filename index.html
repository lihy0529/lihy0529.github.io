About

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at UTNS (UT Austin Networked Systems group), advised by Prof. Aditya Akella and Daehyeok Kim.

My current research interests are networks, architectures, operating systems and machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in designing an efficient network system to boost ML training, as well as using ML methodology to help system design. Our projects often work on programmable switch, smart NIC, FPGA and Linux kernel.

Previously, I spent four fantastic undergrad years in CS Turing Class, Peking University, worked closely with Prof. Tong Yang. My undergraduate research interest lies in databases and theoretical computer science.
